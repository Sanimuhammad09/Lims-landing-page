FAQs

What is the purpose of Fee management software?
The main purpose of fee management software is to make the process of fee collection, segregation, and tracking easy and simple to understand for administrators.
Why use an online fee management system?
The advantage of using a student fee management system online is that it brings all the different processes involved in fee management under a single dashboard. It is precisely segregated and organized to avoid difficulties regarding comprehension.
How can using Fee Management Software make management easier for schools?
Fee management software helps digitize various aspects of school fee management that would otherwise be difficult to do manually. For example, fee collection, checking pending & collected fees, fee report generation, and sending timely reminders can be automated using the software.
What are the benefits of using fee management software for schools?
There are several benefits to the use of fee management software in schools. Firstly, the use of fee management software in schools makes the process of fee management automated. This leads to a lot of processes being taken care of the software itself. A student fee management system also has the advantage of making no computational miscalculations. This makes sure that there are no errors either, hence saving time and effort.
What is the need for an online fee management system today?
With major tasks of organizations being initiated and conducted online, it is no surprise that they are looking towards making every aspect of it follow suit. Fee management involves a lot of steps and takes a lot of time to execute if done manually. In addition, the chances of error are also greater. Therefore, it would be in the interest of the organization to use an online fee management system so that all the fee-related activities can be managed from anywhere.
How can SIMS’s fee management software help manage discounts?
The student fee management system offered by SIMS has a separate section dedicated to making discounts easier to handle. New discounts can be created according to whether the school wants them to be in absolute amounts or percentages. The school administration also has the option to set discounts just for specific fees such as transport fees, library fee, special fee, and so on.
Can fines also be managed with SIMS’s fee management system?
Yes, SIMS has an elaborate system specifically dedicated to fines. The admins can decide on which fee the fine has to be levied and the amount of the fine. In addition to giving them a deadline and the amount of fine payable if the fee is overdue, they also have the option to offer a grace period to pay the fine. The time for the grace period can also be set by the school administration, after which the fine will be administered on a per-day basis.
